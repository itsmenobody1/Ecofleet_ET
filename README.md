ACIA — Autonomous Cost Intelligence Agent

ACIA is an AI-powered enterprise intelligence system that goes **beyond dashboards**. It continuously monitors operational data across SaaS, cloud infrastructure, procurement, tickets/SLAs, and finance invoices to **detect cost leakage**, **quantify the exact financial impact**, and generate **actionable playbooks** that teams can execute through an approval workflow.

Instead of just reporting problems, ACIA answers three critical questions for enterprises:

1. Where is money leaking?
2. How much exactly is being lost? (with math)
3. What steps should be taken to stop it?

---

What ACIA Does

* Detects unused SaaS licenses and renewal optimization opportunities
* Finds idle or underutilized cloud resources and recommends consolidation
* Predicts SLA breaches from ticket signals to prevent financial penalties
* Reconciles invoices to flag duplicate payments and discrepancies
* Produces a live **AI Findings Feed** with quantifiable cost math and step-by-step action playbooks
* Works within a **human approval workflow** before any execution

---

Core AI Agents

* Spend Intelligence Agent** — SaaS, vendors, procurement waste
* Resource Optimization Agent** — Cloud & infrastructure utilization
* SLA Penalty Prevention Agent** — Tickets, deadlines, and escalations
* Finance Operations Agent** — Invoices, payments, reconciliation

Each agent analyzes different data sources and sends verified findings to the central feed.

---

Key UI Sections

* Dashboard — Snapshot of total detected cost leakage and active findings
* AI Findings Feed — Detailed issues with exact ₹ impact and action steps
* Agents — View specialized agents and their roles
* Data Upload — Connect enterprise datasets (CSV/API)
* Impact — ROI and annual savings breakdown
* Approval Workflow — Human-in-the-loop control before execution

---

 Why ACIA is Different

Most analytics tools show charts.
ACIA delivers **intelligence + financial proof + execution guidance**.

Example finding:

> 37 unused Salesforce seats × ₹48,000/seat/year = **₹17.76 Lakhs wasted annually**
> With a 3-step playbook to reclaim seats before renewal.
